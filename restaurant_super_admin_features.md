## Overview
The Super Admin has full control over **one restaurant**. This role manages settings, users, menu, orders, and system-level configurations.

## Core Responsibilities

### 1. Restaurant Management
- Update restaurant profile (name, logo, address)
- Manage opening hours and availability
- Configure basic restaurant settings

### 2. User & Role Management
- Create, update, or remove admins and staff
- Assign roles and permissions

### 3. Menu Management
- Add, edit, or remove categories and items
- Update prices and availability

### 4. Order Management
- View and manage all orders
- Update order status and handle cancellations
- Analytics & Reports
- View sales and order reports
- Track basic performance metrics

### 6. System & Security
- Manage authentication settings
- Control access and permissions

**Note:** Branch and multi-restaurant support may be added in a future phase.
